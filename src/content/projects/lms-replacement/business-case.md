> **Engagement closed 16 December 2022.** Retained on the YAT intranet for reference per the YAT Records Management Policy.

The completed Business Case AccentLoitte delivered in Phase 1 of the LMS Replacement engagement — the options analysis and board-approved recommendation to replace the end-of-supportable-life GrayBoard LMS with DOODLE, deployed on-premises at the Cremorne campus. Approved by the YAT Board in May 2022.

**[Download the Business Case (PDF)](/documents/YAT-LMS-Replacement-Business-Case.pdf)**

The document covers the strategic context, the current-state assessment of GrayBoard, the gap analysis, the options considered and their five-year cost comparison, the risk and impact assessment, the recommendation, and the phased implementation plan — a complete worked example of a board-ready business case.
