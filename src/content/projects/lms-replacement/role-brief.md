> **Engagement closed 16 December 2022.** Retained on the YAT intranet for reference and audit per the YAT Records Management Policy.

This brief set out the roles, responsibilities, and reporting relationships for the AccentLoitte LMS Replacement engagement. The Master Services Agreement (§§4–5, §10) was the authoritative source for engagement scope and governance; this brief was the operational reference held by each role during day-to-day work.

## Engagement context

YAT College engaged AccentLoitte to deliver the end-to-end replacement of YAT's legacy in-house LMS (GrayBoard) with a contemporary commercial LMS platform. The engagement was structured in three sequential Phases:

1. **Phase 1 — Selection and Implementation Plan.** Requirements specification, market evaluation, Replacement LMS recommendation, implementation plan covering deployment, data migration, cutover, and training.
2. **Phase 2 — Deployment and Data Migration.** Procurement and provisioning of on-premises infrastructure at the YAT Cremorne campus; installation and base configuration of the selected Replacement LMS (DOODLE); extract / transform / load of YAT data from the legacy LMS; parallel-running validation; production Cutover.
3. **Phase 3 — Stabilisation and Closure.** Post-Cutover support; user training delivery; as-built documentation and operational runbooks; knowledge transfer to YAT ICT; project Closure Pack and closure sign-off under YAT's Change Management Procedure.

End-to-end delivery (selection through cutover and training) was in scope for AccentLoitte. The scope split is summarised in §3 below and detailed in the Master Services Agreement.

## LMS replacement — senior YAT ICT team recommendations (early 2022)

The following recommendations from senior YAT ICT staff framed the engagement scope:

- The legacy GrayBoard LMS had reached end-of-supportable-life and required replacement rather than further patching.
- A contemporary commercial Learning Management System should be selected to replace GrayBoard, evaluated against YAT's functional requirements, total cost of ownership, RTO-sector fit, and vendor longevity.
- The Replacement LMS should be deployed **on-premises at the YAT Cremorne campus** consistent with YAT's then-current ICT operating model. Cloud hosting was noted as a future consideration but not part of this engagement's scope.
- All YAT student records held in the legacy LMS must be preserved through the migration (consistent with the long-tail student-record retention obligations under the *Standards for Registered Training Organisations 2015*).
- Cutover should occur during the December–January teaching break to minimise impact on active teaching periods.

## Scope summary

The Master Services Agreement (§§4–5) was the authoritative reference for engagement scope. Summary:

**In scope for AccentLoitte**

- LMS requirements specification, market evaluation, and Replacement LMS recommendation (Phase 1)
- Implementation plan covering deployment, data migration, cutover, and training (Phase 1)
- On-premises infrastructure provisioning at the YAT Cremorne campus (Phase 2)
- DOODLE LMS installation and base configuration (Phase 2)
- Data migration from the legacy GrayBoard LMS, including transformation and post-migration verification (Phase 2)
- Production Cutover (Phase 2)
- Post-Cutover stabilisation support (Phase 3)
- User training delivery to YAT staff and trainers (Phase 3)
- As-built documentation, operational runbooks, knowledge transfer to YAT ICT (Phase 3)
- Project Closure Pack (Phase 3)

**Out of scope for AccentLoitte — retained by YAT in-house teams**

- Hardware procurement outside the Phase 1 recommendation (procured by YAT through existing supplier relationships)
- Networking and firewall changes outside the LMS infrastructure boundary (managed by YAT ICT)
- Active Directory account provisioning beyond LMS service accounts (managed by YAT ICT)
- Ongoing operational support of the Replacement LMS after acceptance of the Phase 3 Closure Pack (separately contracted with an external support provider)
- External communications (managed by YAT Marketing)
- End-user support after the stabilisation period
