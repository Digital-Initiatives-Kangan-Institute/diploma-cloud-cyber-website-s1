## ICT Goals

- Ensure reliable ICT services
- Improve ability to respond quickly to changed requirements
- Reduce dependency on in-house server infrastructure
- Implement sustainable ICT practices

## ICT Objectives

- Investigate options for transitioning critical on-premises application services to the cloud by mid-2027.
- Deploy LMS system infrastructure in the cloud before the end of 2027.
- Achieve 99.9% availability for critical systems by the end of 2027.

## Planned ICT Initiatives

To further improve the agility of ICT operations, YAT College plans to utilise the Infrastructure as a Service (IaaS) and Software as a Service (SaaS) models and gradually move suitable on-site systems to the cloud. The LMS has been identified as the first system to be transitioned.

## Alignment with YAT business objectives

The above ICT goals and objectives support YAT's wider business objectives, which include:

- Offering students new ways of learning irrespective of location.
- Expanding throughout Australia.
- Increasing student numbers by 15% annually over the next three years.

The transition of critical application services to cloud-hosted infrastructure is the principal ICT enabler for the location-independent learning and student-growth objectives.
