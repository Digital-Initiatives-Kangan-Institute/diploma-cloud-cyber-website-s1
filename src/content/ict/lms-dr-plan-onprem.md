> The current disaster recovery plan for the on-premises LMS, owned by YAT ICT (v2.3).

The disaster recovery plan for the YAT on-premises Learning Management System — the backup arrangements, the recovery steps, and the recovery objectives the current process achieves. It records a worst-case Recovery Time Objective of 7–11 hours and a Recovery Point Objective of up to 24 hours, both materially short of the ICT Strategic Plan targets (RTO ≤ 4 hours, RPO ≤ 1 hour).

**[Download the Disaster Recovery Plan (PDF)](/documents/YAT-LMS-DR-Plan-OnPrem.pdf)**

Closing this recovery gap is one of the explicit drivers of the LMS cloud migration.
