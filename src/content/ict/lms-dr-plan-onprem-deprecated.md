> **Deprecated.** This disaster recovery plan applies to the former on-premises LMS, which has been migrated to the cloud. It no longer reflects the current platform and must not be relied on for recovery.

The disaster recovery plan that was in force for the on-premises LMS (v2.3). Following the migration of the LMS to AWS, this plan no longer reflects the current platform, its recovery options, or the recovery objectives now achievable. **A replacement disaster recovery plan for the cloud-hosted LMS is required and has not yet been produced** — a DR plan was not included in the original cloud cutover engagement, and that omission is being rectified.

**[Download the Disaster Recovery Plan — Deprecated (PDF)](/documents/YAT-LMS-DR-Plan-OnPrem-DEPRECATED.pdf)**

Retained for reference and as the starting point for the replacement plan.
